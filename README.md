# A Universe of Us

A personal website I made for my girlfriend as a love letter disguised as a storybook. It tells the story of how we found each other — written like a cosmic fairy tale, where we are two stars drifting through the universe until gravity pulls us together.

---

## The Story

The site is split into five chapters, each one a different moment in our story.

---

### Intro — Night Awakening

The story begins in silence. Shooting stars streak across a dark sky and slowly settle. A title fades in, and a single glowing dot pulses at the centre of the screen — like a star waiting to be touched. Clicking it begins the journey.

---

### Chapter I — A Universe Waiting

Before we met, there was just me. This chapter shows a single sun — alone in a vast universe — rotating slowly in space. Words appear beneath it, one line at a time:

> *"Before there was an us, there was just you — and a universe that had not yet found its reason."*

---

### Chapter II — Two Stars Collide

This is the moment we met. A gold star (me) and a violet star (her) drift toward each other, spiral into orbit, and collide in a burst of light. The date we first met appears on screen. Out of that explosion, new stars are born — the beginning of everything.

---

### Chapter III — The Solar System of Us

After the collision, our story becomes a whole solar system. Each year of our relationship is a planet orbiting a central star. Clicking a planet opens that year's memories: photos and notes from moments that mattered — our first real memories together, quiet days, celebrations.

---

### Chapter IV — Still Counting

The final chapter. Two stars dance together in a figure-eight — endlessly looping, always returning to each other. A love letter appears line by line. Below it, a counter counts every day we have been together — still going up.

The last line reads:

> *"And we are only just beginning."*

---

## About This Site

This was made as an anniversary gift — not a card, not a playlist, but something she could open and feel the whole story at once. Every word, every memory, and every photo in it is ours.
